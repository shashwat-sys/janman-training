About This Module

This training module was developed by Janman People's Foundation as part of the Jan Nyaya Abhiyan legal fellowship programme. It is designed for law students, legal fellows, and junior advocates engaging with Dalit and Adivasi communities in Biar and across India.

The module covers the SC/ST (Prevention of Atrocities) Act 1989 as amended by the 2015 and 2018 Amendment Acts, the SC/ST (Prevention of Atrocities) Rules 2016, and the body of Supreme Court and High Court jurisprudence through 2024.

For case assistance, legal research, or training inquiries, contact Janman at shashwat@janmanindia.org or WhatsApp the helpline.

Legal Framework Covered
This module covers the complete legislative and judicial landscape of SC/ST protection law
1955
Protection of Civil Rights Act
Penalises enforcement of untouchability; civil rights including temples, wells, education
1989
SC/ST (Prevention of Atrocities) Act
Core statute with 22 original atrocity offences; special courts and relief provisions
1995
SC/ST (PoA) Rules
Rule 12 victim relief; DVMC composition; investigation timelines; charge-sheet deadlines
2016
Amendment Act
Added 19 new offences; Sec 18A mandatory arrest; no anticipatory bail; strengthened Sec 15A victim rights
2018
SC Decision — Prathvi Raj Chauhan
Supreme Court upheld constitutionality of Sec 18A; restored mandatory arrest; reversed dilution
Art. 17
Constitutional Foundation
Abolition of untouchability; Art. 15, 16, 46 — non-discrimination, reservation, upliftment

⚡ What's Next for You as an SC/ST Act Practitioner

🏛️

Appear Before Special Courts

Apply your knowledge in Special Court proceedings — as SPP, as defence counsel, or as amicus in complex cases. Every real case deepens your mastery of the Act.

📋

File a Compensation Enforcement Petition

Identify a case in your district where s.17 compensation has not been paid. File a writ of mandamus before the HC — this is among the highest-impact actions you can take immediately.

📡

Monitor NCSC / SHRC Orders

Subscribe to updates from the National Commission for Scheduled Castes and your State Human Rights Commission. Their orders on SC/ST Act violations create strong precedents for district-level litigation.

📢

Train Paralegals & Community Workers

Use the Jan Nyaya Resource Centre's paralegal and social worker track materials to train grassroots workers on FIR rights, compensation entitlements, and emergency reporting — multiplying the impact of your legal skills.

⚔️

Connect with Other SC/ST Act Lawyers

Join the Jan Nyaya Abhiyan network of district lawyers and legal fellows working on atrocities cases across Bihar. Coordinate on systemic PILs and share case strategies.

📱 Join the Jan Nyaya Legal Network

Connect with Janman People's Foundation's team for case support, co-counsel arrangements, and legal resources on SC/ST Act cases across Bihar and India.

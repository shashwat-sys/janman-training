This Module Is For You If…

You are a Paralegal Volunteer (PLV), community worker, or social worker engaged with SC/ST communities in Bihar. You don't need a law degree to use this module — it uses plain language and real examples from ground-level work.

You will learn to recognise when the SC/ST Act applies, what to do immediately, how to help a victim navigate the FIR process, how to write a complaint, and when and how to connect the victim to a lawyer or legal services authority.

⚖️ Quick Law Reference

Section 3(1)
Atrocity offences against SC/ST persons — 22 sub-clauses
Section 3(2)
Aggravated offences — enhanced punishment if committed by public servants
Section 4
Neglect of duty by public servant — mandatory prosecution
Section 9
Investigation must be by Dy.SP rank or above — no lower officer
Section 14
Special Courts — mandatory, one per district, exclusive jurisdiction
Section 15A
Victim rights — information, protection, travel expenses, rehabilitation
Section 18A
No bail, no preliminary inquiry, no prior sanction for arrest
Rule 12
Compensation schedule — amounts and payment timelines (3 days, 2 months)
Rule 17(3)
DVMC — District Vigilance and Monitoring Committee for enforcement
Article 17
Abolition of untouchability — enforceable fundamental right
Article 226/32
HC/SC writ jurisdiction — mandamus, compensation (Rudul Shah)
§156(3) CrPC
Magistrate's power to direct police to register FIR and investigate

ℹ️ About Janman People's Foundation

Janman People's Foundation is a Bihar-based human rights organization working under the Jan Nyay Abhiyan initiative. We work on issues of gender-based violence, disability rights, social security, hate crime documentation, and access to justice for marginalized communities across Bihar and India.

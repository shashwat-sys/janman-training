🏛️ Why Constitutional Literacy Matters

India's Constitution guarantees every citizen fundamental rights enforceable in court. Yet millions remain unaware of these protections. Janman's constitutional law series equips lawyers, paralegals, activists, and citizens to understand, assert, and litigate these rights — transforming constitutional text into lived justice.

🧭 About This Programme

Who Should Enrol

Lawyers, paralegal volunteers, law students, social workers, community organisers, and citizens who want to understand and enforce constitutional rights.

What You Will Learn

From the basics of Part III to advanced PIL drafting strategies — grounded in real Supreme Court and High Court judgments from Bihar and across India.

Certificates

Earn a certificate at each level (L1, L2, L3) and a comprehensive final certificate on completing all three. All certificates are digitally verifiable.
